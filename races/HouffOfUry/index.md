## Race Notes

### 2018

Houff Of Ury Hill Race

Date: Sunday, 15 July 2018

Time: 11.30

Venue: Mackie Academy, Stonehaven

Distance: 8km

Climb: 132m

Notes:

The race is organized by Stonehaven Running Club in association with Stonehaven Highland Games.

Stonehaven Mackie Academy playing field (games arena)

Registration from 10:00 at games entrance, race starts at 11:30
