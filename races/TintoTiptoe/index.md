## Race Notes

### 2018

**Tinto Tiptoe Hill Fun Run **

**Saturday November 10th 2018 ** **Enter at Start Car Park** ****

** **

**Under UKA Rules. Scottish Athletics Licence.**

** **

**Directions**

 | ** **

**Fun Run Register & Start ** **(Grid Ref: 963 373) ** ****

** Turn off the Lanark to Abington road A73 at minor cross- roads nearest Tinto Hill. (On your right, if heading south.) **

** ** ** **

**Entry Fee**

 | ** **

**10p ** (Pensioners half price.)

**Start**

 | ** **

**2.07 Approx.**

** **

**Course**

 | ** **

**Distance 1.2k. Climb 50m.**

** **

** Run (or walk) up track, round hill fort and back. **

**Under 8 and over 98 must be accompanied.**

** **

** Note: There are no facilities at the start. **

**The nearest are 3 miles away at Symington.**

** **

** Bring plenty of warm & waterproof clothing. **

** **

**Prizes**

 | ** **

**None. **

** **

** Certificates juice & Kit Kat to all smiling finishers. **

** **

**Note**

 | ** **

** The Tiptoe is held in conjunction with the Tinto Hill Race 6.5k / 450m, which starts at 2pm, and the tough junior race 3.2k / 180m, starting at 2.05pm. **

** **

** Registration for both these is 3 miles away in Symington village hall off A72. **

**(GR 996 352, OS Landranger sheet 72) ** ****
