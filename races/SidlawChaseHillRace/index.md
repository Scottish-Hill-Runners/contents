## Race Notes

### 2018

Start time: 3 pm

Registration from 2pm at Balkello Community Woodland car park. Grid Ref. 367 385

Entry – FREE

Over 17 age

This is a new hill race which starts at Balkello Community Woodland Car Park below the Sidlaw hills, just north of Dundee. The route climbs Craigowl hill, then Balkello hill and then Auchterhouse Hill. The route then doubles back following the same route to Craigowl Hill and then descends back to the car park. There will be check points at each hill summit.

The race is 10km in length with a total climb of 445m

There are no prizes, but results will be published in the local press and will be sent to Scottish Hill Runners

For more information, please contact Dave Hanlon at [kingofthehills@sky.com](mailto:kingofthehills@sky.com) or call 07714 221628
