## Race Notes

### 2018

**Sidlaw Sunset Chase Race**

Wednesday 12th Sept 2018

Race starts at 7pm Sunset is 7:38 pm

Registration from 2pm at: Balkello Community Woodland car park. Grid Ref. 367 385 Post code DD3 0QY

Entry – FREE

Over 16 age

This is a new hill race which starts at Balkello Community Woodland Car Park below the Sidlaw hills, just north of Dundee. The route climbs Craigowl hill, then Balkello hill and then Auchterhouse Hill. The route then descends back to the car park. There will be check points at each hill summit.

The race is 7.5 km in length with a total climb of 380m

There are no prizes, but results will be published in the local press and will be sent to Scottish Hill Runners

For more information, please contact Dave Hanlon at kingofthehills@sky.com or call 07714 221628
