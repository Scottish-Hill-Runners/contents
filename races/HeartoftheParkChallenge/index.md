## Race Notes

### 2019

2019 Race date confirmed as Sunday 16th June.

### 2018

The Heart of the Park Challenge is a multi terrain trail run, set in the immediate area around Braemar and run over a 12 km course.

Runners will follow a waymarked, undulating course in some of the UK's most spectacular scenery in the heart of the Cairngorms National Park. We will get you wet, then we'll get you clarted in mud and then we'll get you wet again as you run through mire, through rivers and through pine forest. And if that doesn't leave you out of breath, then the views will.

Please note the 'Heart of the Park Challenge' will not take place during the same weekend as the 'Junior Highland Games', as it has in previous years.

Entry Fee £10 until 15th May. Then £12 until 15th June. Then £15 until race day.

Entry limit 150 max.

For further details, contact secretaryski@outlook.com or write to Mr. D.Evans, Taste, Chapel Brae, Braemar AB35 5YT.
