Wins for Jacob Adkin (Moorfoot Runners) and Nancy Scott (Edinburgh AC) at the Ben Ledi Uphill race on Saturday 11th April.

This was the Scottish Athletics Senior and U20 Hill Running Championship so Kieran Hardie (Harmeny) and Emily Atkinson (Haddington) were the winners in the U20 categories.

The race route was shortened slightly due to very strong winds on the top.
