**Parking:** 

* Craibstone Golf Course Car Park (please use overflow parking - like the January El-Brim-Ick Race)
* Please be considerate to the Craibstone Golf Course Members and their carpark.
* Please note the Golf Course may be hosting tournaments in April and May and do expect possibly a higher use of the car park should the tournaments continue into the evening.

**Permissions:**

* This race would not be possible without the generous permissions from Aberdeen City Council and Craibstone Golf Course. Thank you.
