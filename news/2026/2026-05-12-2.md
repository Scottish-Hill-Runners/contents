**Following the recent announcement of a new SHR Championship Relay, to be held at Comrie this year, please see below the criteria for this and any future SHR Championship relays.**

We would greatly welcome interest from any club in running a future relay event, so please do get in touch.

## SHR Relay Championships – Criteria

### Course & Format Requirements

* Minimum of **3 legs**
* All legs must be **consecutive stages**
* At least **one paired leg**
* Each stage must be between **4 and 10 miles**
* At least **50% of the legs** must be:
  * Category A
  * Require navigation

***

### Prize Categories

**Awards are given as follows:**

* **Open, Senior Women (SW), Mixed**
  * Mixed teams must be at least **50% female**
  * **1st, 2nd and 3rd place** awarded
* **Open V40, FV40**
  * **1st, 2nd and 3rd place** awarded
* **Open V50, FV50, Open V60, FV60**
  * **1st place only** awarded as a minimum

***

### Event Capacity & Entries

* **Minimum capacity:** 50 teams
  * Must ensure adequate car parking
* **Initial entry limit:**
  * Maximum of **4 teams per club**
* **Additional entries:**
  * Allowed after a specified date, subject to availability

***

### Hosting Future Events

* Clubs wishing to host future relay championships must:
  * Submit an **Expression of Interest (EOI)** to the SHR committee
  * **Deadline:** 31st October
* A one-off club relay event is also encouraged
