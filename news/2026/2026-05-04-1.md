**SCOTTISH HILL RUNNERS JOURNAL 2025/26**&#x20;

CALL FOR SUBMISSIONS \*EXTENDED DEADLINE\* Another year, another journal, once again Scottish Hill Runners are looking for submissions for the 2025/26 Journal! As usual we will be looking for ARTICLES, REPORTS and POETRY on hill running, hill races, training for the hills, being in the hills, getting off the hills, hill landscapes, hill people and everything in between. If you don't fancy writing, we're also looking for PHOTOS (candid, staged, Photoshop, your choice) and any ARTWORK you'd like to share. The deadline for submission will be Sunday 14th June. All submissions to be sent to [committee4@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:committee4@scottishhillrunners.uk) If you have an idea you'd like to discuss please don't hesitate to get in touch. We look forward to hearing from you! New Editor Needed - If you're interested in making a contribution to the work of the SHR we are looking for a new Journal editor for 2026/27. If this piques your fancy please email the address above for more information.
