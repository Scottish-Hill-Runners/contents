Online entry is open for the Glas Tulaichean Uphill race on Saturday 16th May 

Entry Central Link - [Glas Tulaichean Uphill Race - EntryCentral](https://www.entrycentral.com/GT2026)

The Glas T Toe Tickle Fun Run will also be on

 

Details on the Facebook page here - [Glas Tulaichean Uphill Race 2025 | Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/events/698589252848208)  and below

 

**SATURDAY 16<sup>th</sup> MAY 2026 at 1pm**

 

Distance: 7.2km / 4.5mi    Ascent: 670m / 2200ft    (Cat AS)

Host club: Dundee Hawkhill Harriers

£8 / £6 Scottish Athletics members

 

**Registration / Number collection:** 11am – 12:40pm at Dalmunzie Estate, Spittal of Glenshee, PH10 7QE

 

Uphill only route to the summit of Glas Tulaichean (1051m) on good double track with a few stream crossings & the last few hundred meters to the top on open hill. A land rover will take a bag to the top for each competitor so when you finish you can collect your warm clothes, pause to enjoy the spectacular views (on a clear day!) & jog back down for post-race tea & prize-giving. Please bring your own cups for tea & coffee to cut down on plastic waste.  Pre-entry will help the organisers in planning for numbers but entries will also be available on the day from 11:00 & will close at 12:40pm (Bag drop closes at 12:20 to allow time to transport your gear to the top). **You must carry full waterproof body cover including hat & gloves and entries are restricted to over 17s.**

 

**In the event of poor weather an alternative course may be run at the Race Organiser’s discretion.**

There is also the \~1km Glas T Toe Tickle fun run at 1:30pm for any younger runners who wish to take part.  Entry is free & all runners will receive a certificate & treat.

Our thanks to Dalmunzie Estate for supporting this event. 

Prize categories:      M/F  1<sup>st</sup>, 2<sup>nd,</sup> 3<sup>rd</sup> / 1<sup>st</sup> V40 / V50 / V60 / V70 etc

1<sup>st</sup> team Male / Female (3 to count)

 

The event is organised by Dundee Hawkhill Harriers. The race is bound by SAL and Scottish Hill Runners' rules. Minimum age is 17. All hill race competitors have a prime responsibility for their own safety. You must judge as to whether you are able and equipped to take part. This is a risk sport.

 

**All runners taking part are required to carry full body cover including hat & gloves.**

 

For full safety details go to:   [**http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Safety.aspx**](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Safety.aspx)

 

**Online entries close on Thursday 14th May 2026 at 9pm.  There will also be entry on the day.**
