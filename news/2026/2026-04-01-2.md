**SHR Hill Running Relay Championships**

The SHR are introducing a hill running relay championship in 2026. The inaugural event will be on Sunday 29 August at the Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay. The SHR committee hope to host this championship each year going forward and will be asking clubs interested in hosting these championships to put in an expression of interest before October each year. The event can be an existing relay or a one-off event.

Further details will appear on the website soon. The SHR will award SHR championship prizes to the winning teams.
