# Insurance

Source: https://scottishhillrunners.uk/Insurance.aspx

## Introduction

Public liability cover has been obtained for the 2022/23 season, from 19 September 2022 to 19 September 2023. The page describes this as a privilege of membership with no extra charge.

## The Policy

The Scottish Hill Runners policy provides:

- GBP 5 million public liability insurance
- GBP 1 million professional indemnity insurance

The cover is for members, officers and volunteer helpers in connection with their hill running activities.

The cover is through:

- Hiscox Insurance Company Limited
- Hiscox Underwriting Limited

## Who Is Covered

The cover applies to:

- Race organisers who are members of SHR
- Race competitors who are members of SHR
- Identified race marshals and other helpers who are not members of SHR

The policy is restricted to permanent UK residents. Local UK law applies. Validity is UK-wide. An excess of GBP 250 applies to third-party property damage.

Policy schedule link:
https://scottishhillrunners.uk/Download.aspx?Item=SCOY36CS01%20Policy%20Schedule-1.pdf

## Scope and Limitations

The policy only applies to hill races in the conventional sense. It does not apply to adventure races such as 2-day mountain marathons, or to multi-disciplinary events such as bike-run events.

Race organisers must be SHR members and must sign a declaration that they will abide by the safety rules published by Scottish Hill Runners. The page states that non-compliance will jeopardise claims and will be investigated by the committee.

Non-compliance with safety rules by competitors must also be treated very seriously. Members may inspect the policy details and seek clarification from the SHR Committee in the first instance.

## Race Organiser Insurance Form

Race organisers wishing to use SHR insurance for their race should complete a safety sign-off document. The page says it is available as:

- Word file:
  https://scottishhillrunners.uk/Download.aspx?Item=Insurance%20form%2020232024.docx
- PDF:
  https://scottishhillrunners.uk/Download.aspx?Item=Insurance%20form%2020232024.pdf

It must be returned to SHR for validation at least four weeks before the event.
