## Overview

Runners must be under 23 on race day and complete any 3 out of 5 races.

@Schedule

## Rules

- Eligibility: Under 23 on race day.
- Awards eligibility: Scottish eligibility is required (Scottish by birth, residence, or first-claim Scottish club).
- Scoring: Best 3 races count.
- Points: 41 for 1st, 39 for 2nd, 38 for 3rd, etc. (leader bonus point included).
- Tie-break: If runners are level on cumulative score, better aggregate score over the three counting races wins; if still tied, the position is shared.

## Awards

- Prizes to first 3 men and first 3 women.
- Mementos for SHR members who complete at least 3 races.
