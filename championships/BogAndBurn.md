## Overview

The Bog & Burn Series consists of Wednesday night hill races across Central Scotland during the summer months. The best 6 results count.

@Schedule

## Rules

- Scoring: Points are awarded for finishing position in each race.
- Series score: Aggregate score is calculated from each runner's best 6 results.

## Awards

A prize-giving is held after Caerketton, the final race in the series.
