## Overview

Scottish Hill Runners (SHR) have run this championship since 1983 as an all-round hill running competition. For 2026, the championship uses 6 races, with each runner’s best 4 results counting.

For categories under 60, those 4 counting races must include:

- 1 Short
- 1 Medium
- 1 Long
- 1 additional race from any category

For 60+ categories, competitors may complete any 4 of the 6 races.

## 2026 race schedule

- Ben Chonzie (Long) - 18 April
- Durisdeer (Medium) - 30 May
- The Law Breaker (Short) - 20 June
- Glamaig (Short) - 11 July
- Two Breweries (Long) - 26 September
- Run Of The Mill (Medium) - 24 October

## Key rules

- Navigation: GPS devices must not be used as a navigational aid. Competitors must carry and be able to use a physical map and compass.
- Minimum age: 18+ on race day.
- Eligibility for awards: Scottish eligibility is required (by birth, residence, or first-claim Scottish club membership).
- Membership: Non-affiliated individuals can compete; SHR membership is needed for championship completion mementos.

## Categories

Individual categories:

- Senior Men/Women
- Over 40 Men/Women
- Over 50 Men/Women
- Over 55 Men/Women
- Over 60 Men/Women
- Over 65 Men/Women
- Over 70 Men/Women (if entrants)

Age category is determined by age on race day, with mid-season age-category transitions handled from birthday onward.

## Team competition

- Prizes for first men’s and women’s teams in senior and veteran categories.
- Counting team scores:
  - Senior Men: 4 to count
  - Women: 3 to count
  - Men’s Over 40: 3 to count
  - Women’s Over 40: 3 to count
- Athletes can only count for one club team per championship race (the team entered on the day).

## Scoring and tie-break

- Points are awarded to the top 40 in each category.
- First place receives 41 points (includes 1 bonus point), second 39, and so on.
- Tie-break: head-to-head record across championship races; if still level, competitors tie.

## Race cancellation policy

If a championship race is cancelled:

1. SHR will try to re-organize the same race within the calendar year.
2. If not possible, SHR will seek a suitable replacement race of the same category (S/M/L).
3. If neither is possible, the championship may be decided on best 3 results from 5 races (instead of 4 from 6), with eligibility restrictions clarified on the championship record.

## Awards

- First three in each category receive medals.
- Category winner receives the Scottish Hill Running Trophy (held for one calendar year).
- SHR members who complete the championship receive a memento.
